Latin Americanist Meetups

Informal gatherings for scholars of Latin American politics

Who we are

A Space to Connect

Latin Americanist Meetups (LAM) is a committee that organizes informal social gatherings at APSA and MPSA. Our goal is to create a relaxed and inclusive space for scholars doing research on Latin America to connect, meet new people, and continue conversations outside of panels and sessions. We meet every Friday of the conference.

How it works

At Every APSA & MPSA

We organize a happy hour at a venue near the conference hotel.

  • This is an inclusive, drop-in event — everyone is welcome to attend
  • No RSVP required
  • Each person covers their own drinks and food
  • Feel free to stop by, circulate among colleagues, and stay as long as you'd like
